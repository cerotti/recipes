Cacio e pepe (or just cum)

This is a very yummy pasta, it's almost a carbonara but a bit lighter and it looks like it's made with cum (it's not)

Difficulty:medium
Time:30-40min
Kcal:bro it's not for a diet I'm sorry
Servings: 4 people

Ingredients

  • 400g Spagott pasta
  • black pepper
  • 200g pecorino cheese (no you stupid dumb fuck you cannot use another type of cheese coz it's not cacio e pepe then)

Steps

  1. Put on the fire a pot with water to cook the pasta: fill it with about half of the water you usually use, this way it will be richer in starch. In the meantime, grate the pecorino
  2. Transfer the pecorino in a bowl, keeping some aside for serving.
  3. When the water has come to a boil, salt moderately and dip the spaghetti
  4. Grind the pepper if it's not already and put it in a non stick pan and toast it on low heat. Pour just a bit of the boiling water
  5. Take off the spaghetti 2-3mins before the time showed on the package and put them in the pan but keep some of the water
  6. Finish cooking them in the pan while stirring
  7. Now prepare the pecorino cream. Finely grate the pecorino in a bowl and then add a bit of the boiling water you used for the pasta. Whip vigorously
  8. Pour the cream in the pan when it's creamy but a bit pasty
  9. With low heat, adjust with more pecorino or more water for respectively more pastiness or more creaminess
  10. Serve with the remaining pecorino on top. You can add more pepper if you wish. If you have truffles, add a bit on top for extra wealth
